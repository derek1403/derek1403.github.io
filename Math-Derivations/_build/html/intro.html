歡迎來到 PC 的筆記本：那些被「略過」的細節#

歡迎各位讀者。

建立這個筆記本的初衷源於我在學習過程中常遇到的一個痛點：課本或論文中那些常被 一筆帶過 的數學概念與推導。

在這裡我嘗試將這些缺漏的拼圖補齊，我不追求繁複華麗的數學技巧而是力求 「簡單」 與 「邏輯清晰」，在每一個證明區塊中我會明確地採用以下結構：

  1. 【已知】(Preliminaries)：我們先擁有哪些工具

  2. 【假設】(Assumptions)：我們在什麼樣的前提下進行討論

  3. 【證明】(Proof)：若您同意上述兩點，那麼我們如何順理成章地推導出結果

這裡會有什麼內容？#

我不拘泥於純數學領域，內容將涵蓋：

  • 數學原理：課程中那些 「顯然可得」 但其實需要一番推導的小知識

  • 物理推導：補完那些被跳過的物理計算步驟

  • 程式技巧：數據處理相關的實用邏輯與技巧

關於 AI 協作與我的信念#

在這個筆記本中許多內容是經過我與 AI 深度討論後的產物，但我必須強調 AI 是我的助手而非我的槍手。

我對 AI 產出抱持著 「攻防」 的態度，我會盡全力去質疑、攻擊 AI 的推導過程；如果它產出的內容無法通過我的知識審查，我將反覆詰問。

「在高等教育中我們討論的真理，很大程度上取決於『能不能說服人』。」

只有當 AI 的解釋能夠在邏輯上完全說服我並挺過我所有的反思與質疑時，我才會採納並將其整理於此，這是我使用 AI 的信念也是我對這些筆記負責的方式。儘管如此，錯誤在所難免若您發現任何謬誤非常歡迎指正。

數值驗證#

我經常使用 Python 。除了理論推導，我會盡可能在結論後附上 Python 程式碼驗證，我希望透過數值模擬的方式，讓這些理論不只停留在符號運算而是能在數據上被接受、被看見。

希望看完這些證明的你們，未來在面對那些跳過無數過程的課本、論文或敘述時，能有一種豁然開朗的通透感。

Welcome to PC’s Notebook: Uncovering the “Skipped” Details#

Welcome, readers.

The motivation behind this notebook stems from a common frustration I encountered during my learning journey: those mathematical concepts and derivations in textbooks or papers that are often “glossed over” or dismissed as trivial.

Here, I attempt to piece together these missing puzzles. I do not aim for overly complex or flamboyant mathematical techniques; instead, I strive for simplicity and logical clarity. For every proof presented, I adhere to the following structure:

  1. Preliminaries (Knowns): What tools do we already possess?

  2. Assumptions: Under what premises are we conducting this discussion?

  3. Proof: If you accept the above, here is how we logically derive the result.

What to Expect?#

I do not limit myself to pure mathematics. The content will cover:

  • Mathematical Principles: “Obvious” classroom knowledge that actually requires rigorous derivation.

  • Physics Derivations: Filling in the missing calculation steps often skipped in physics problems.

  • Programming Techniques: Practical logic and techniques related to data processing.

AI Collaboration and My Philosophy#

Many contents in this notebook are the result of deep discussions with AI. However, I must emphasize that AI is my assistant, not my ghostwriter.

I adopt an “adversarial” attitude towards AI-generated content. I do my best to question and attack its logic. If its output cannot pass my knowledge scrutiny, I will question it repeatedly.

“In higher education, the truth we strive for is largely grounded in the ability to persuade.”

I only adopt and organize concepts here if the AI’s explanation can logically convince me and withstand my rigorous reflection. This is my conviction in using AI, and it is how I take responsibility for these notes. Nevertheless, errors are inevitable. If you find any fallacies, your feedback is highly appreciated.

Numerical Verification#

I am a heavy Python user. Beyond theoretical derivations, I will, whenever possible, include Python code for verification after the conclusions. I hope that through Numerical Simulation, these theories will not just remain abstract symbols but become tangible and accepted through data.

I hope that after reading these proofs, you will find a moment of clarity when facing those textbooks, papers, or narratives that skip countless steps.
