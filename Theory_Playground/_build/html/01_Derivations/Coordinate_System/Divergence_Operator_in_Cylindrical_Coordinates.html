Divergence Operator in Cylindrical Coordinates (圓柱座標中的散度算子)#

證明目標:#

對於任意向量場 \(\mathbf{A} = A_r\hat{r} + A_\lambda\hat{\lambda} + A_z\hat{z}\)，證明其散度為：

\[ \nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial[r A_r]}{\partial r} + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \]

假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【已知 1】 圓柱座標的 \(\nabla\) (Nabla) 算子定義： $\(\nabla = \hat{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial r} + \hat{\lambda}\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda} + \hat{z}\frac{\partial}{\partial z}\)$

  • 【已知 2】 圓柱座標單位向量的直角座標轉換：

    直角座標的單位向量 \((\hat{I}, \hat{J}, \hat{K})\) 是絕對固定的常數，但圓柱座標的單位向量 \((\hat{r}, \hat{\lambda}, \hat{z})\) 會隨著角度 \(\lambda\) 變化：

    • \(\hat{r} = \cos\lambda \hat{I} + \sin\lambda \hat{J}\)

    • \(\hat{\lambda} = -\sin\lambda \hat{I} + \cos\lambda \hat{J}\)

    • \(\hat{z} = \hat{K}\)

  • 【已知 3】 單位向量的偏微分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial \lambda} & \frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial z} \\ \frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial \lambda} & \frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial z} \\ \frac{\partial \hat{z}}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial \hat{z}}{\partial \lambda} & \frac{\partial \hat{z}}{\partial z} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & \mathbf{\hat{\lambda}} & 0 \\ 0 & \mathbf{-\hat{r}} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

    利用【已知 2】，我們對 \(\hat{r}\) 和 \(\hat{\lambda}\) 取偏微分：

    • \(\frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial \lambda} = -\sin\lambda \hat{i} + \cos\lambda \hat{j} = \mathbf{\hat{\lambda}}\) (這代表徑向向量轉動時，變化量的方向切好是切線方向)

    • \(\frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial \lambda} = -\cos\lambda \hat{i} - \sin\lambda \hat{j} = \mathbf{-\hat{r}}\) (這代表切線向量轉動時，變化量的方向是指向圓心的)

  • 【已知 4】 基底是正交的：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{bmatrix} \hat{r} \cdot \hat{r} & \hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{r} & \hat{z} \cdot \hat{r} \\ \hat{r} \cdot \hat{\lambda} & \hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{\lambda}& \hat{z} \cdot \hat{\lambda} \\ \hat{r} \cdot \hat{z} & \hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{r} & \hat{z} \cdot \hat{z} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

證明:#

我們將 \(\nabla\) 算子與向量 \(\mathbf{A}\) 做內積 (Dot Product)。這是一條主線，我們只需要耐心把分配律展開：

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} &=&& \left( \hat{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial r} + \hat{\lambda}\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda} + \hat{z}\frac{\partial}{\partial z} \right) \cdot \left( A_r\hat{r} + A_\lambda\hat{\lambda} + A_z\hat{z} \right) \\ &=&& \hat{r} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial r} \left( A_r\hat{r} + A_\lambda\hat{\lambda} + A_z\hat{z} \right) \\ &&+& \frac{1}{r} \hat{\lambda} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda} \left( A_r\hat{r} + A_\lambda\hat{\lambda} + A_z\hat{z} \right)\\ &&+& \hat{z} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \left( A_r\hat{r} + A_\lambda\hat{\lambda} + A_z\hat{z} \right)\\ &=&& \hat{r} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r}\hat{r} + A_r\frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial r}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial r}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda\frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial r}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial r}\hat{z} + A_z\frac{\partial \hat{z}}{\partial r}\right) \right] \\ &&+& \frac{1}{r} \hat{\lambda} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \lambda}\hat{r} + A_r\frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial \lambda}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda\frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial \lambda}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial \lambda}\hat{z} + A_z\frac{\partial \hat{z}}{\partial \lambda}\right) \right]\\ &&+& \hat{z} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial z}\hat{r} + A_r\frac{\partial \hat{r}}{\partial z}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial z}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda\frac{\partial \hat{\lambda}}{\partial z}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}\hat{z} + A_z\frac{\partial \hat{z}}{\partial z}\right) \right] \\ &\overset{\text{已知 3}}{=}&& \hat{r} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r}\hat{r} + A_r0\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial r}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda 0\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial r}\hat{z} + A_z 0\right) \right] \\ &&+& \frac{1}{r} \hat{\lambda} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \lambda}\hat{r} + A_r\hat{\lambda}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda (-\hat{r}) \right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial \lambda}\hat{z} + A_z 0\right) \right]\\ &&+& \hat{z} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial z}\hat{r} + A_r 0\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial z}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda 0\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}\hat{z} + A_z 0\right) \right] \\ &=&& \hat{r} \cdot \left[ \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r}\hat{r} + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial r}\hat{\lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial r}\hat{z} \right] \\ &&+& \frac{1}{r} \hat{\lambda} \cdot \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \lambda}\hat{r} + A_r\hat{\lambda}\right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda}\hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda (-\hat{r}) \right) + \left(\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial \lambda}\hat{z} \right) \right]\\ &&+& \hat{z} \cdot \left[ \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial z}\hat{r} + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial z}\hat{\lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}\hat{z} \right] \\ &=&& \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r}\hat{r} \cdot \hat{r} + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial r}\hat{r} \cdot \hat{\lambda} +\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial r}\hat{r} \cdot \hat{z} \\ &&+& \frac{1}{r} \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \lambda}\hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{r} + A_r\hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{\lambda} \right)+ \left( \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda}\hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{\lambda} + A_\lambda \hat{\lambda} \cdot (-\hat{r}) \right)+ \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial \lambda}\hat{\lambda} \cdot \hat{z} \right]\\ &&+& \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial z}\hat{z} \cdot\hat{r} + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial z}\hat{z} \cdot\hat{\lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}\hat{z} \cdot\hat{z} \\ &\overset{\text{已知 4}}{=}&& \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r} + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial r}0 +\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial r}0 \\ &&+& \frac{1}{r} \left[ \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \lambda}0 + A_r \right)+ \left( \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} + A_\lambda 0 \right)+ \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial \lambda}0 \right]\\ &&+& \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial z}0 + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial z}0 + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \\ &=&& \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r} + \frac{1}{r} \left[ A_r + \frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} \right]+ \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \\ &=&& \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r} + \frac{1}{r} A_r + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \\ &=&& \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r} + \frac{1}{r} A_r \right)+ \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \\ &=&& \frac{1}{r} \left(r\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial r} + A_r \right)+ \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \\ &=&& \frac{1}{r} \frac{\partial[r A_r]}{\partial r}+ \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_\lambda}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z} \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]
